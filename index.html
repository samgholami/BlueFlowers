Marjan Farsad

She was born in 1983 in Tehran, Iran. Marjan Farsad is an animation filmmaker, illustrator, singer, and songwriter. Her debut album “Blue Flowers” was released in 2014.Like most of her generation, she left Iran after the green revolution in 2009. Her songs are mostly about immigration, love, and hope, and she is the creator of all of her music videos.

The simplicity of lyrics and music in cooperation with a sad and modern feel of them made her very popular between the young population in Iran as well as Iranian all around the globe.
